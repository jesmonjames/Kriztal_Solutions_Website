Our Story

Welcome to our company

With humble beginnings in a little corner office, Kriztal solutions first put down roots in 2016, with a small team of passionate programmers and designers fresh out of college, and a vision. A vision of putting their skills and vision to good use and coming up with products in which functionality and innovation are part and parcel. Not long into our first year, we signed with Biolands Pvt. Ltd to design a customised product for their cocoa market known as Biolands Field Information System (BFIS). After working hand in hand with the team at biolands we successfully implemented the software. We next set our sights on creating an all encompassing business solutions software, to cater to all the needs of a business, and allow businesses to collect and store their data in a manner that is easy to access and understand. Our efforts culminated in the creation of the software- Aaspada that was successfully released in 2018. Kriztal continues to work on projects that are a blend of both seamless design and superior function and hopes to continue growing with these key factors never changing.

Aaspada


Aaspada, named for the sanskrit word for business or power, holding true to its name, Aaspada encompasses a complete business solution to fully equip and assist your business.

Aaspada deals primarily with stock checking, billing, and reports. Allowing you to seamlessly organise and analyse your data, on the user friendly and elegantly designed interface.We also provide various reports which are integral part of any business firm.

  • Stock Reports.

CUSTOM MADE SOLUTIONS


BFIS

BFIS, The Biolands Field Information System, was one of the first customizable softwares that Kriztal Solutions had undertaken for Biolands Pvt Ltd., Tanzania. The software is tailored towards the cocoa industry, with features such as: logging of buyers, sources of raw material, amount of raw material bought, etc. The holistically equipped system also helps the business manage data with the help of the efficient data-manipulation software

  • Ageing reports
  • Monthly Periodic reports: helping track the buying of cocoa from villages.
  • Daily reports
  • Commission sheets & Commission pay slips: BP / price wise Quantity.

The BFIS system is fully tailored to the customers needs, and designed immaculately, with our team working in conjunction with our customers tirelessly, to produce a quality product that would provide a superior user experience.
